  • 1860

    In the 1860s, the term “snowflake” was an insult used by abolitionists of Missouri to describe people who still supported slavery.

  • 1970

    The term was used in the 1970s by Americans as an insult toward Black people perceived to be acting too similar to white people.

  • 2016

    The term was used in the 2016 presidential election by Donald Trump and right-wing conservatives to insult left-wing liberals for being “fragile and easily offended”.
